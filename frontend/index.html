Piece Breakdown

P - President

The Mission President holds the most authority. He can move horizontally, vertically, and diagonally.

A - Assistant

Assistants help support the President. They can teleport to any of the eight positions around the President.

Z - Zone Leader

Zone Leaders can go on exchanges with the District Leader. They can also move horizontally or vertically.

D - District Leader

District Leaders can go on exchanges with any Missionary. They can also move diagonally.

S - Sister Training Leader

Sister Training Leaders can also go on exchanges with any Missionary. They can move horizontally and vertically, as well.

M - Missionary

Missionaries can move one step in any direction. But be careful - they must always stay in a companionship, adjacent to another member of their team!

The Goal

Eliminate all members of the opposing team, or force them to have no available moves. Good luck!
